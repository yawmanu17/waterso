Water Distribution Loss Detection and Network Management Platform

## Overview

OFORI — Field-to-Desk DMA Mapping & NRW Analyzer is a web-based digital platform designed to assist water utilities in monitoring distribution system performance and reducing Non-Revenue Water (NRW) using District Metered Area (DMA) analysis.

The application integrates field meter-reading operations, geospatial infrastructure mapping, billing analytics, and system performance dashboards to provide utilities with a comprehensive view of water distribution networks. By connecting field data collection with centralized engineering analysis, the platform supports early identification of water losses, infrastructure inefficiencies, and abnormal consumption patterns.

---

## Key Capabilities

• District Metered Area (DMA) network monitoring  
• Non-Revenue Water (NRW) calculation and analysis  
• Geospatial infrastructure and customer mapping  
• Field meter-reading interface with route navigation  
• Customer billing and consumption analytics  
• Pump station energy monitoring and analysis  
• Infrastructure asset mapping (tanks, reservoirs, pump stations, valves)  
• System performance dashboards and reporting  
• Utility data export for engineering analysis

## Application Modules

### System Performance Dashboard
Provides an overview of system input volume, billed consumption, NRW calculations, and operational indicators for distribution system performance.

### DMA Network Mapping
Interactive GIS interface showing customer locations, infrastructure assets, and distribution system layout.

### NRW Analysis Module
Allows utilities to evaluate water losses by comparing system inflow volumes with billed consumption and authorized usage.

### Field Meter Reading Interface
Supports meter-reading operations by allowing field staff to enter customer readings and transmit data to centralized analysis.

### Billing and Revenue Analytics
Analyzes customer consumption data and provides billing verification and revenue monitoring tools.

### Pump Energy Monitoring
Evaluates energy consumption of pumping systems using measured electrical demand or hydraulic performance estimates.

## Infrastructure Impact

Water distribution systems across the United States experience significant losses due to aging infrastructure, leakage, and operational inefficiencies. Non-Revenue Water represents a major challenge for municipal utilities and increases both operational costs and energy demand.

OFORI — Field-to-Desk DMA Mapping & NRW Analyzer provides a digital framework that helps utilities monitor system performance, detect water losses, and improve operational efficiency through data-driven infrastructure management.
